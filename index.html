About Me.

Work Experience

After earning a B.S. in Zoology from Weber State University, I spent 5 years studying the nesting success of shorebirds around the Great Salt Lake. This job taught me valuable teamwork and communication skills. After having children, I was primarily a stay-at-home mom, but worked part-time in retail while taking prerequisite courses to prepare for the dental hygiene program at Weber State. However, when the pandemic hit, I realized the risks associated with working in a dental office were too high for me. That's when my sister, a software developer, suggested I look into coding, and now I'm pursuing that path. I'm excited to be here!
